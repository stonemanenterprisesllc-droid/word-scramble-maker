How to make a printable word scramble

Enter up to 25 words in the box above — one per line or separated by commas — or click a theme button for a ready-made word list. Press Make My Puzzle and your word scramble appears instantly with every word shuffled. Press Print Puzzle and your browser prints a clean worksheet: the puzzle on page one and the answer key on page two, with no buttons, menus, or ads on the printout. If you'd like a different shuffle of the same words, click Re-scramble.

Who uses this word scramble generator?

  • Teachers — turn this week's spelling or vocabulary list into a five-minute warm-up worksheet.
  • Parents and homeschoolers — practice spelling with words your child is actually learning.
  • Party planners — make a birthday, baby-shower, bridal-shower, or holiday game in seconds.
  • Activity directors — large-print-friendly puzzles for classrooms, camps, and senior centers.

Tips for a better puzzle

Words of 5–9 letters scramble best: long enough to be a challenge, short enough to stay fun. Avoid 3-letter words (too easy) and very long phrases. For younger kids, check UPPERCASE letters in Options — capital letters are easier to recognize. A themed list (all animals, all Christmas words) gives solvers helpful context, which is why our theme buttons group words that belong together.

How does the scrambler work?

Each word's letters are shuffled randomly right in your browser — nothing you type is sent to any server. The tool re-shuffles until the scrambled version differs from the original, so a word never appears unscrambled by accident. Your answer key preserves the original order so grading takes seconds.
